MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Contract ID: MSA-2024-ACME-001 · Effective Date: January 15, 2024

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024 ("Effective Date"), by and between Acme Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 100 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Client"), and Henderson Music Group, a California limited liability company ("Provider").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Provider shall provide Client with music distribution, licensing, and rights management services as described in Schedule A attached hereto ("Services"). Provider shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards.

2. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of three (3) years ("Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8. Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods.

3. COMPENSATION

In consideration of the Services, Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in Schedule B. Payment shall be due within thirty (30) days of receipt of Provider's invoice. Late payments shall bear interest at the rate of 1.5% per month.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All intellectual property rights in the Works shall remain with the Licensor. Client is granted a non-exclusive license to distribute the Works within the Territory as specified in Schedule A.
