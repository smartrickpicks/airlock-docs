What does “Role Linkage” mean and why is it flagged?
Role Linkage maps each contract party to their operational role in your system (e.g., Owner → Licensor, Distributor → Payee).

It’s flagged because no linkage was detected in this agreement. Without it, accounting routing and payment allocation can’t be automated.

🔗 Role Linkage → START HERE
How should I resolve it?
Click the START HERE badge on the Role Linkage field to begin. You’ll need to:
1. Confirm the Owner entity
2. Map it to a Salesforce account
3. Assign the operational role

Once linked, the field status will change from REVIEW to PASS and accounting rules will activate.
